Fashion can seem superfluous, but it is evolved from a basic need. Its utilitarian origins have proven a fertile ground for beauty and self-expression.

The background of this page is Margiela's SS 'Artisinal' 2017 Show (link). It references deconstruction, social media, isolated communities, and pioneers. It's a push-and-pull between past and present, connected and alone, polished and in-progress. What does it tell us that humans are so ready to create beauty and meaning from a need to stay warm?
