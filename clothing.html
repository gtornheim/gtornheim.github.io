Fashion seems superfluous to many, but it's archetypical human-centered desgin. I've been inspired by designers who bring abstract ideas from architecture, history, geopolitics and design to a personal level.

My favorite designers are Raf Simons, Demna Gvasalia, and Martin Margiela. The background to this video is Margiela's SS 'Artisinal' 2017 Show (link). Deconstruction, social media, isolated communities, and pioneers are all referenced. It's a push-and-pull between past and present, connected and alone, polished and in-progress.
