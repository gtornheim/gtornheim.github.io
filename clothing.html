Fashion can seem superfulous and indulgent to an outsider (and probably to some insiders, too), but if you concentrate on a few designers that you like, they can introduce you to ideas and bring abstract ideas from architecture, geopolitics, and design to a personal level. I've been inspired by fashion for years and I've picked up some skills like sewing and flat pattern design along the way.

My favorite designers are Raf Simons, Demna Gvasalia, and Martin Margiela.
